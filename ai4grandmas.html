In computer science, artificial intelligence (AI), sometimes called machine intelligence, is intelligence demonstrated by machines, in contrast to the natural intelligence displayed by humans and animals.

Deep learning is a branch of machine learning algorithms inspired by the structure and function of the brain called artificial neural networks. Some of the deep learning architectures are Deep neural networks, deep belief networks, recurrent neural networks and convolutional neural networks

Artificial Intelligence can be defined as a program that can sense, reason, act and adapt. Machine learning, generally means algorithms whose performance improve as they are exposed to more data over time. Deep learning is subset of machine learning in which multi-layered neural networks learn from vast amounts of data.

Deep learning is an artificial intelligence function that imitates the workings of the human brain in processing data and creating patterns for use in decision making. Deep learning is a subset of machine learning in artificial intelligence (AI) that has networks capable of learning unsupervised from data that is unstructured or unlabeled. Also known as deep neural learning or deep neural network.
